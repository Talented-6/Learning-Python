> 寻找水仙花数
```Python
for num in range(100, 1000):
    low = num % 10
    mid = num // 10 % 10
    high = num // 100
    if num == low ** 3 + mid ** 3 + high ** 3:
        print(num)
```
> 反转正转数
```Python
num = int(input('num = '))
reversed_num = 0
while num > 0:
    reversed_num = reversed_num * 10 + num % 10
    num //= 10
print(reversed_num)
```
